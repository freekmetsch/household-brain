Icon colour shotgun

Round 3 — poster iconography. Eight new sets rethink the drawing itself after Tom Haugomat's Huttopia posters: sun-behind-subject, framed mini-posters, dusk bands, vastness, lake reflections, day/night splits, cast shadows, and stencil knockouts (Enamel's colours, new geometry). Round 2's colour systems follow below. Each card mirrors the real app contexts: bottom nav, utility glyphs, food grid, plus a dark-background strip. Also live in-app at /design/icons with one-tap apply.
